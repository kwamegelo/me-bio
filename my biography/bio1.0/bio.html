About me

Someone may ask, who is Angelo?
Angelo Kwame Amoateng was born on December 29,2006, at St. Michael's Hospital in Pramso in the ashanti region to Mr. Daniel Tabi and Ms. Elisabeth Asare. His early education begun at Agape Academy, where he completed primary school. He continued his academic journey at High Academy Annex for Junior High School and later attended Opoku Ware School (OWASS) for his Senior High school education.

Currently, Angelo is pursuing his studies at the prestigious Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology(KNUST) With a strong interest in mathematics and problem-solving, Angelo finds joy in tackling complex equations. In his leisure time, he enjoys listening to music, which serves as both a source of relaxation and inspiration. Although he has yet to begin his career or achieve notable milestones, Angelo is a determined and passionate individual who is eager to make a mark in his chosen field in the future.
